SKillet PIZZA!!!

Description

Issa pizza with the crust made from cheese. ion about this one, it ain't gonna look right when you cook it.

ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded part-skim mozzarella
  • 5 sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespons of torn FRESH basil leaves
  • 4 small moz balls

Directions

  1. cook mozzeralla in 10-inch skillet for 2-3 minutes
  2. arrange other ingredents around the cheese leaving room for a crust to form
  3. remove from heat and attempt to cut into eight slices. Let cook for a little while.
