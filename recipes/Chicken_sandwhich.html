"Heavenly" CHICKEN GARLIC SANDWHICH

Description

Im about tried of these werid looking recipes clogging my VM's digital hard drive, but for real for real this looks like something I would actually smash down my gullet. mainly because i'm a huge THOT for both garlic and chicken.

ingredents

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 5 garlic cloves minced
  • 4 hoagie rolls split
  • 2 pounds of cubed chicken breast(probably easier to cube them boys yourself)
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzeralla cheese

directions(I do not know how to spell)

  1. preheat oven to 365F
  2. melt butter with garlic in a bowl in 30-second intervals
  3. place hoagie on tray and butter it up, then evenly place chicken on the bread
  4. cook in oven for 10-20 minutes, or until cheese is melted
